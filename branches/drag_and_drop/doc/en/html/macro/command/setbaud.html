setbaud

Causes Tera Term to change speed of serial port. 

setbaud <value>

Parameters

integer <value> (less than version 4.66)
The corresponding ID of speed. 
value    speed (bps)
1        110 
2        300 
3        600 
4        1200 
5        2400 
6        4800 
7        9600 
8        14400 
9        19200 
10       38400 
11       57600 
12       115200 
13       230400 
14       460800 
15       921600
integer <value> (version 4.66 or later)
The immediate speed.

Example

; change speed to 115200 bps

; less than version 4.66
setbaud 12

; version 4.66 or later
setbaud 115200
