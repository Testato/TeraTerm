until, enduntil

Repeats. 

until <expression>

  ...

  ...

enduntil

Remarks

Repeats the statements between 'until' and 'enduntil' while <expression> is zero.

Example

; Repeat ten times.
i = 1
until i > 10
  i = i + 1
enduntil
