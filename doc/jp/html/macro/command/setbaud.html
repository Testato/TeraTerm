setbaud

setbaud <value>

value    baudrate

2        300 
3        600 
4        1200 
5        2400 
6        4800 
7        9600 
8        14400 
9        19200 
10       38400 
11       57600 
12       115200 
13       230400 
14       460800 
15       921600

例

setbaud 12            ;change baud rate to 115200
