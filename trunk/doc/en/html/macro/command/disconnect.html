disconnect

Closes a connection. 

disconnect [<intval>]

Remarks

Closes the communication between Tera Term and the host.
If Tera Term is not terminated by this command, the link between Tera Term and MACRO is kept.

When the <intval> is `0', the Tera Term is immediately disconnected without confirmation.
When the <intval> is not `0', the confirmation dialog is shown whether the Tera Term disconnects.
